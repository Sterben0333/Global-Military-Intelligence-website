ABOUT THE PLATFORM

Global Military Intelligence

A comprehensive, open-source defense analysis platform providing real-time military data, live conflict tracking, and strategic intelligence for researchers, analysts, and enthusiasts worldwide.

🌍

Nations Database

Detailed military profiles for 33+ nations including army, navy, and air force data with equipment inventories and personnel counts.

⚔️

Live Conflicts

Real-time tracking of global conflicts with interactive maps, intensity ratings, and live news feeds from verified sources.

📊

Compare Tool

Side-by-side comparison of military capabilities between nations — personnel, budget, equipment, and nuclear status.

📡

Real-Time News

Aggregated defense news from 11+ verified military sources filtered by AI to exclude non-defense content.

🗺️

Interactive Maps

Leaflet-powered world maps with military power markers, nuclear proliferation routes, and conflict zone overlays.

📜

Military History

Explore major historical wars with interactive conquest maps, timelines, alliance networks, and territorial control visualizations.

📁 Data Sources

  • GlobalFirepower (GFP) — Annual military rankings
  • IISS Military Balance — Equipment & personnel data
  • SIPRI — Defense expenditure database
  • Federation of American Scientists — Nuclear arsenals
  • ACLED — Armed conflict & event tracking
  • Official government defense publications
